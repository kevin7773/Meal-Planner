## Sunday, July 05 - Turkey and Cheese Deli Sandwich Night

**Recipe:** FDP-0042 rev 1 (candidate)
**Planned Diners:** 4

## Recipe Card

- **Active prep:** 15 minutes
- **Cook time:** 0 minutes
- **Cooking method:** No-cook
- **Estimated fiber:** 7 grams per serving
- **Kid-friendly design:** Both children like/love it
- **Best seasons:** Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter
- **Schedule fit:** No-cook dinner for extreme heat

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 8 slices whole-grain sandwich bread
- 12 ounces deli turkey
- 8 slices cheddar or provolone cheese
- 8 ounces potato chips
- 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 2 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 2 cups baby carrots
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons yellow mustard, served on the side

### Seasonings

- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

## Directions

1. Arrange the bread, deli turkey, cheese, and yellow mustard so everyone can assemble a sandwich without mayonnaise.
2. Combine the romaine, cucumber, tomatoes, and baby carrots in a serving bowl.
3. Whisk the olive oil, apple cider vinegar, dried oregano, kosher salt, and black pepper.
4. Serve the vinaigrette on the side of the garden salad.
5. Serve each sandwich with potato chips and garden salad.

## Leftover Plan

- Refrigerate deli ingredients and undressed salad separately for lunch the next day.
