# Seasonal Rules

## Summer: June Through August

Favor outdoor grill, smoker, Blackstone, slow-cooker, minimal-cook, and no-cook
meals. Do not suggest soup. Avoid chili, heavy casseroles, long indoor oven
meals, and heavy baked pasta unless specifically requested.

Prioritize corn, tomatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, cucumbers, berries, peaches,
watermelon, green beans, and fresh herbs.

Use Blackstone variety such as hibachi chicken, fried rice, chicken or turkey
cheesesteak bowls, fajitas, smash tacos, breakfast-for-dinner, stir fry,
burrito bowls, and turkey burger bowls.

## Fall

Favor sheet-pan meals, chili, soups, and pasta bakes.

## Winter

Favor slow-cooker meals, comfort food, casseroles, and roasts.

## Spring

Favor lighter bowls, grilled chicken, salads, and fresh vegetables.
