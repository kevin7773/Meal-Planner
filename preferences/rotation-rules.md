# Rotation and Reuse Rules

- Never repeat the previous week's exact lineup.
- Avoid repeating the same main dish within 2-3 weeks when practical.
- Rotate cooking methods, proteins, and flavor profiles.
- Revisit family favorites without making consecutive weeks feel alike.
- Include at least 2-3 intentional leftover or ingredient reuse connections.

Useful reuse patterns include:

- Fajita chicken into tacos, bowls, or fried rice
- BBQ chicken into lunches, wraps, potatoes, or sliders
- Bell peppers and onions across fajitas, bowls, skewers, and tacos
- Brown rice across grain bowls and fried rice
- Pinto beans across Mexican Monday, sides, bowls, and salads
- Seasonal fruit as a side on multiple nights
