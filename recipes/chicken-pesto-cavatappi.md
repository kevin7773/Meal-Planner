# Chicken Pesto Cavatappi

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded chicken (rotiserie chicken works best)
- 1 tablespoon Salt
- 1 pound cavatappi pasta or other small pasta
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 large tomato, cored and diced
- 4 ounces mushrooms, sliced (optional)
- 1/4 cup chicken broth or vegetable broth
- 1/4 cup dry white wine
- ? cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup homemade or store-bought pesto
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. In a large saucepan or stock pot, bring 4 quarts of water and salt to a boil. Add pasta and cook until al dente, about 10 to 12 minutes. Drain well.
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add tomatoes and mushrooms (if using) and cook for about 5 minutes, until some of the liquid has been released and the tomatoes are heated through.
3. Stir in the broth, wine, and cream and bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium and cook until the sauce has thickened slightly, about 2 minutes.
4. Add pesto and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
5. Add pasta and toss until uniformly coated in the sauce. Transfer to a serving dish or individual plates and garnish with Parmesan cheese.
6. Notes:
7. - You can substitute rotini, rigatoni, bow-tie, or any small or medium-sized pasta for cavatappi.
8. - Noodles & Company includes mushrooms, but they are optional.
9. - Choose a dry white wine such as Pinot Grigio or Chardonnay, or substitute with more chicken broth.
10. - Homemade pesto can be made with fresh basil, parsley, olive oil, garlic, parmesan cheese, and a nut like pine nuts or walnuts. Store-bought pesto is also fine.
11. - This recipe yields about 8 cups of pasta, enough for 8 first-course or side dish servings or 4 generous bowls.
12. - Store leftovers covered in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
