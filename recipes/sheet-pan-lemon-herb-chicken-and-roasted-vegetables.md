# Sheet Pan Lemon Herb Chicken and Roasted Vegetables

## Recipe Card

- **Cook time:** 25 minutes
- **Cooking method:** Stovetop

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 2.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into 1.5-inch pieces
- 2 lbs small red potatoes, quartered
- 2 bell peppers (any color), cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 large red onion, cut into wedges
- 2 cups broccoli florets
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 lemons, one thinly sliced, one juiced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tsp dried oregano
- 1.5 tsp dried thyme
- 1 tsp paprika
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. Preheat oven to 400?F (200?C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Whisk to combine.
3. Add the chicken pieces, potatoes, bell peppers, and red onion to the bowl. Toss until everything is evenly coated with the herb mixture.
4. Spread the chicken and vegetables in a single layer on a large baking sheet (you may need two baking sheets to avoid overcrowding, which can steam the vegetables instead of roasting them).
5. Arrange the lemon slices among the chicken and vegetables.
6. Roast for 20 minutes.
7. Remove the baking sheet(s) from the oven, stir the ingredients, and add the broccoli florets. Toss gently to coat the broccoli.
8. Return to the oven and roast for another 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through (internal temperature of 165?F/74?C) and the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.
9. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired, and serve immediately.
