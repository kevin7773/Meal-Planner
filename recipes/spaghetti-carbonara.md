## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
- 4 large eggs
- 100g grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and then drain the pasta.
2. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the pancetta or guanciale until crispy. Add the minced garlic and saut? for about 1 minute.
3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and Parmesan cheese. Season with a pinch of salt and a generous amount of black pepper.
4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
5. Remove the skillet from the heat and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly to coat the pasta and create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed to reach desired consistency.
6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.
