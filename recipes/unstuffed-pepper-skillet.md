# Unstuffed Pepper Skillet

## Recipe Card

- **Active prep:** 10 minutes
- **Cook time:** 25 minutes
- **Cooking method:** Stovetop
- **Estimated fiber:** 8 grams per serving
- **Kid-friendly design:** Not kid friendly - for the parents only
- **Best seasons:** Spring, Fall, Winter
- **Schedule fit:** Imported candidate; review before scheduling

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon Onion powder
- 1 teaspoon Garlic powder
- Salt & black pepper to taste
- 1 green bell pepper, diced
- 1 red bell pepper, diced
- ? yellow onion, diced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 14.5 ounce petite diced tomatoes, drained
- 15 ounces tomato sauce
- 2 cups instant white rice
- Beef broth (salted kind)

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the olive oil, diced peppers, and onions. Stir until pieces are coated in oil and allow them to saut? for 5 minutes, occasionally stirring.
2. Add the ground beef and cook until no pink is visible within the meat.
3. Add the minced garlic and allow it to cook for an additional 30-45 seconds.
4. Drain off any excess grease and season with onion powder, garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
5. Add drained petite diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, instant rice, and beef broth. (Follow the rice to water ratio on the box for how much broth to add).
6. Bring to a boil and reduce heat to low, cover, and allow to simmer until rice is cooked thoroughly and the broth is absorbed.
7. Serve & enjoy!
