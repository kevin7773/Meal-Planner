# Chicken Cheesesteak Baked Potatoes

## Recipe Card

- **Active prep:** 20 minutes
- **Cook time:** 20 minutes
- **Cooking method:** Blackstone and microwave
- **Estimated fiber:** 10 grams per serving
- **Kid-friendly design:** Cheesy chicken and baked potatoes with vegetables optional
- **Best seasons:** Spring and summer
- **Schedule fit:** Any day except Mexican Monday

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, thinly sliced
- 4 large russet potatoes
- 2 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 large yellow onion, sliced
- 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 pound green beans, trimmed

### Seasonings

- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

### Toppings and Sides

- 4 slices provolone cheese
- 2 cups mixed berries

## Directions

1. Pierce the potatoes and microwave for 12-16 minutes, turning halfway, until
   fork-tender.
2. Mix the smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, kosher salt, and black
   pepper.
3. Toss the chicken, bell peppers, onion, and mushrooms with the olive oil and
   seasoning mixture.
4. Cook on a medium-high Blackstone for 10-12 minutes until the chicken reaches
   165 F and the vegetables are tender. Keep vegetables separate when desired.
5. Steam the green beans for 5-6 minutes. Split each potato, fill with chicken,
   and top with provolone. Serve with green beans and berries.

## Leftover Plan

- Extra chicken filling can become lunch quesadillas.
