# Crock Pot Sesame Chicken

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pound boneless/skinless chicken breasts
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons dried onion
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 1 tablespoon oil
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons cornstarch dissolved in 3 tablespoons water
- Sesame seeds

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. Put the chicken into the crock pot.
2. Combine honey, soy sauce, onion, ketchup, oil, and garlic in a bowl.
3. Pour this mixture over the chicken in the crock pot.
4. Put the lid on and cook on low for 3-4 hours or on high for 1 1/2 ? 2 1/2 hours, or just until the chicken is cooked through.
5. Remove the chicken from the crock pot, leaving the sauce.
6. Dissolve 2 teaspoons of cornstarch in 3 tablespoons of water and pour into the crock pot.
7. Stir to combine with the sauce.
8. Replace the lid and cook the sauce on high for 10 minutes more or until slightly thickened.
9. Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces and return to the crock pot.
10. You can leave the chicken in and simmer on low or serve immediately.
11. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve over rice.
