# Mom's Wonton Soup

## Recipe Card

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups water
- 2 teaspoons Garlic Better Than Bouillon
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 bag of frozen chicken/vegetable mini wontons
- 2 baby bok choy, chopped

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. Bring the chicken broth and water to a boil.
2. Turn down the heat to a simmer.
3. Add the mini wontons and chopped bok choy.
4. Cook for 5-6 minutes.
5. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
