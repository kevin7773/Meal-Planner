## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 2 cups cooked pasta
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 5 cloves of chopped/sliced garlic
- 2 tbsp crushed red pepper flakes
- fresh parsley
- lots of grated Parmesan

### Seasonings

## Directions

1. - coat bottom of deep pan with oil and heat
2. - stir in garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook til aromatic
3. - add cooked pasta, parsley, and grated cheese. Stir.
4. - Serve!
