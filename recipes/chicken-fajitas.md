# Chicken Fajitas

## Recipe Card

- **Active prep:** 20 minutes
- **Cook time:** 12 minutes
- **Cooking method:** Blackstone
- **Estimated fiber:** 12 grams per serving with listed sides
- **Kid-friendly design:** Familiar soft tacos with mild seasoning and toppings served separately
- **Best seasons:** Spring and summer
- **Schedule fit:** Mexican Monday

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced
- 3 bell peppers, sliced
- 1 large yellow onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 8 whole-wheat fajita tortillas
- 1 (15-ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

### Seasonings

- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

### Toppings and Sides

- 1 cup salsa
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 lime, cut into wedges
- 4 cups watermelon wedges

## Directions

1. Mix the chili powder, ground cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion
   powder, kosher salt, and black pepper.
2. Toss the chicken, bell peppers, and onion with the olive oil and seasoning
   mixture.
3. Cook on a medium-high Blackstone for 10-12 minutes, turning regularly, until
   the chicken reaches 165 F and the vegetables are tender.
4. Warm the pinto beans and whole-wheat tortillas.
5. Divide the chicken and vegetables among the tortillas. Serve with pinto
   beans, salsa, Greek yogurt, lime wedges, and watermelon.

## Leftover Plan

- Double the chicken and vegetables when planned for later-week bowls or fried
  rice; record the doubled quantities in that week's grocery list.
