# Chicken Sausage Broccoli Orzo

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 12 oz chicken sausage, sliced into rounds
- 1 cup orzo pasta
- 2 cups broccoli florets (fresh or frozen)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 1/4 cups low-sodium chicken broth
- 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional for creaminess)
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chicken sausage and cook until browned, about 4?5 minutes. Remove and set aside.
2. In the same skillet, add garlic and orzo. Toast the orzo for 1?2 minutes, stirring constantly.
3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a simmer. Stir in the broccoli florets.
4. Cook uncovered for 8?10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until orzo is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.
5. Stir in Parmesan cheese, heavy cream (if using), and red pepper flakes. Return the chicken sausage to the skillet.
6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
