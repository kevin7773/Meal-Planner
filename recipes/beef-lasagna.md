## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 9 lasagna noodles
- 2 cups ricotta cheese
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
- 1 egg
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Directions

1. Preheat your oven to 375?F (190?C).
2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut? until fragrant.
3. Add ground beef to the skillet, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
4. Stir in marinara sauce and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and a pinch of salt.
6. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain.
7. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
8. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture, half of the mozzarella, and a third of the meat sauce.
9. Repeat the layers, ending with noodles and remaining meat sauce.
10. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
11. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
12. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
