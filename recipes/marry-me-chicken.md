# Marry me chicken

## Ingredients

### Main Ingredients

- For the Chicken
- 1/2 pound bacon
- 3 large skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
- 1 (16-ounce) package angel hair pasta
- For the Sauce
- 1 cup chicken broth, divided
- 1 1/2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 3/4 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes

## Directions

1. Arrange the bacon in a large, cold skillet in a single layer. Turn the heat to medium and cook, flipping halfway, until crisp.
2. While the bacon cooks, slice the chicken breasts in half lengthwise and pound them to even thickness. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
3. Remove the bacon from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, melt the butter, add the chicken, and cook until browned and cooked through, about 4-5 minutes on each side.
4. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
5. Remove the chicken and cover loosely with foil.
6. Add the garlic, oregano, and thyme to the skillet. Pour in half of the chicken broth and scrape the brown bits.
7. Sprinkle the flour over the broth and whisk until smooth. Slowly pour in the remaining broth, followed by the cream, whisking constantly until smooth and thick.
8. Add the Parmesan in small handfuls, stirring well to melt.
9. Add the sun-dried tomatoes and red pepper flakes. Return the chicken and crumbled bacon to the pan. Stir until coated and cook for 1-2 minutes.
10. Serve the chicken and sauce over the pasta with chopped parsley for garnish.
