### Main Ingredients

- 3 lb Russet Potatoes, thin sliced
- 1 Medium White Onion, chopped
- 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1 tsp Pepper
- 1 tsp Paprika
- 1 tsp Parsley
- 3 tbsp Oil
- 2 lbs Kielbasa Sausage, cut into ? inch chunks
- 2 Medium Zucchinis, cut into ? inch chunks
- 1 Red Bell Pepper, cut into ? inch chunks
- ? cup Italian Dressing

### Seasonings

- Review imported ingredients for seasoning classification

## Directions

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine potatoes, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, oil, parsley, and paprika.
2. In another mixing bowl, combine kielbasa, zucchini, bell pepper, and Italian dressing.
3. Heat griddle to medium-high heat. Add potato mixture and flatten out to almost a single layer. Cook for 5 minutes, then do your best to flip it all over and cook another 5 minutes.
4. Add in sausage mixture and combine with potato mixture while spreading back out into a mostly single layer. Let cook for 5 minutes on each side.
5. Remove from griddle and let sit for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with your favorite hot sauce or maybe a fried egg.
